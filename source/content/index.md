Hello world!

These are my personal study notes, I use [obsidian](https://obsidian.md/)  to organize them for myself which works well enough, but that makes them extremely difficult to share via `theentiretyofmyknowledge.pdf` or similar file formats. Obsidian-links are pretty essential to my kind of my *~~organisational~~*-style but they do not transfer well in any kind of file format. So here is a website with fuzzy finding.

> [!Question] Why "Quarry"?
> Obsidian, Quarz, MNTN ... the name "Quarry" for a place to get my notes kinda fit.

> [!Info] How to use this site
> If you want to find something, **use the search option**. It indexes page names, the content and tags.
> Don't just rely on these notes to take you through the course, use [AnkI](https://apps.ankiweb.net/) or another [FSRS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaced_repetition) for learning stuff by heart. Engage with the material your Professor(s) offer to you and if you are *really* stuck, ask them. I have done that many times and have always gotten answers that at least point me in the right direction.
> So this is not a "read this once and Uni is free" course or whatever. It's a collection of notes, just on a website for ease of use. You still need to do the work yourself, if my organization style helps you with that, great. Use it.
